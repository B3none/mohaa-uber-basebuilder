<><><>Info<><><>

The jetpack is a trigger that attaches a jetpack to your back. You are able to lift off, fly around and shoot people with your own guns.

<><><><><><><><><><>

<><><>Controls<><><>

Jump and hold Use Key - Lift off the ground.
Hold Use key (when in the air) - Propel yourself forward.

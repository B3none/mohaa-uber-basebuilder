<><><>Info<><><>

When you press your use key on the dog trigger (it looks like a dog with a green light under it, and the dog barks) you will become a living breathing dog! Press your firing keys to bite people, and eat them up for doggie dinner!

<><><><><><><><><><>

<><><>Advantages<><><>

You're a dog, as you can see. When you bite someone, it deals 100 damage to them, meaning...you guessed it, they die!

<><><><><><><><><><>

<><><>Disadvantages<><><>

You don't have any weapons when you are a dog. But if you find the all weapons trigger, you can become "the dog with a gun!"

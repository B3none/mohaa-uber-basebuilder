<><><>Info<><><>

The giant trigger is simply a trigger that turns you into a scale 10 giant. Everyone will literally see you as "the titan that killed us all!" You will be 10 times bigger than you normally are.

<><><><><><><><><><>

<><><>Advantages<><><>

Your large size will be incredibly hard for someone to hurt you with a rifle or a machine gun.

<><><><><><><><><><>

<><><>Disadvantages<><><>

Your large size will be easily noticed. Anyone with grenades, a bazooka, or anything that makes an explosion will easily kill you. It is also hard to build when you are a giant.

<><><>Info<><><>

The Guided Missile is a little missile that you fly around in and blow people up. When you crash into someone, they will explode.

<><><><><><><><><><>

<><><>Controls<><><>

Look Left, Right, Up, and Down - Steer the missile.
Left Click - Fire your missile in a straight line into someone.

<><><>Info<><><>

Capture The Church is a mod in which you capture the church. This mod is on The Crossroads. Basically its a non-basebuilder mod, and you team tries to capture the church the most time. The team with the most points wins.

<><><><><><><><><><>

<><><>How To Play<><><>

You basically go to the church, go into the room near the top of the church, and stay in the room with the radio playing the corny music. You stay in there for 25 seconds. For every 25 seconds, you get a point for you team.

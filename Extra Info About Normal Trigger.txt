<><><>Info<><><>

The normal size trigger is pretty much self explanatory. If you are a midget or a giant, you can take the normal size trigger to become normal size. When you kill someone they might think, "Wow I got killed by someone that wasn't even a midget or a giant. What does that make me then?"

<><><><><><><><><><>

<><><>Advantages<><><>

You will be able to build normally when you are building.

<><><><><><><><><><>

<><><>Disadvantages<><><>

Your normal size will be an everyday-of-playing-mohaa target for everyone else.

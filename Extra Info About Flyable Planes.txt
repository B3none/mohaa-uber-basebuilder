<><><>Info<><><>

The flyable plane is something that turns you into a plane, and you are able to fly around and shoot people with it.

<><><><><><><><><><>

<><><>Controls<><><>

Use Key - Switch between MG42 gun, 30CAL gun, and Rockets. 
Right Click - Drop bombs (although it normally doesn't work, for some reason). 
Move your mouse up, down, left, and right - Steer the plane.
Left Click - Fire your currently selected weapon.
W - Speed up.
S - Slow down.
A - Barrel roll Left.
D - Barrel roll right.
Space Bar - Jump out of plane.
